# Initial Concept
Individual learners looking for self-paced, interactive education.

# Product Vision: Bodh
Bodh is an AI-powered educational platform designed for individual learners seeking a self-paced and highly interactive learning experience. The core philosophy is "Learning through Intelligent Conversation," moving beyond passive video consumption to an active, engaged learning process guided by an intelligent AI companion.

## Target Audience
- **Individual Learners:** Students and lifelong learners who prefer self-directed education but desire the guidance and interaction of a personal tutor.

## Strategic Goals (6-Month Horizon)
- **High User Engagement:** Optimize the AI companion's conversational accuracy and responsiveness to ensure a natural and helpful tutoring experience.
- **Adaptive Learning Outcomes:** Successfully implement assessment systems that adapt to the user's progress, providing a clear path to mastery and measurable improvement in learning retention.
- **Content Management Excellence:** Build a powerful administrative suite for seamless course, module, and lesson orchestration.

## Key Features
- **Admin Dashboard:** A robust administrative interface for content creators to manage courses, structure modules, and upload lesson materials.
- **Real-time Voice AI Tutoring:** Seamlessly integrated voice-enabled AI that provides instant explanations, answers questions, and guides learners while they watch video content.
- **Generative Video-Based Quizzes:** Dynamically created assessments generated from specific video segments to reinforce learning and identify areas needing further clarification.
- **Personalized Adaptive Learning Paths:** An intelligent curriculum that evolves based on the learner's conversation history, quiz results, and demonstrated understanding of the material.
